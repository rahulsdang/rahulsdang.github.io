📁 Case Studies

Documented examples of real problems identified and solved with specific actions and measurable outcomes.

SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
Systems

ATS Migration: Greenhouse to iCIMS Plus Workday HRIS Integration

Problem

SCS Engineers had no real-time visibility into hiring pipeline health or retention data across 70+ offices and 44 states. Greenhouse was not integrated with their HRIS. Leaders were making workforce decisions without accurate data. Agency relationships had no accountability structure, costing significant spend annually with no measurable ROI.

Action

Led the full transition from Greenhouse to iCIMS including configuration, data migration, and hiring manager training across all offices. Implemented Workday HRIS integration, unifying hiring, onboarding, and retention data for the first time. Launched a revamped employee referral program and introduced SeekOut for passive sourcing to reduce agency dependency across 44 states.

Result

Agency spend reduced by $250K+. Leadership gained first-ever real-time pipeline and retention visibility. Internal sourcing through SeekOut and referrals became a meaningful channel across a national footprint.

SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
Process

Intake Process Redesign: Interview-to-Offer Ratio from 5:1 to 3:1

Problem

No structured intake process existed. Hiring managers submitted reqs without clear requirements, compensation alignment, or interview frameworks. The result was a 5:1 interview-to-offer ratio: five candidates going through the full process for every single hire. Expensive in time, hiring manager bandwidth, and candidate experience across a 70-office national firm.

Action

Redesigned the intake protocol end-to-end. Implemented mandatory kickoff calls before any req went live. Built standardized job brief templates capturing requirements, compensation range, non-negotiables, and interview format. Established 48-hour feedback expectations with hiring managers. Introduced SeekOut for passive sourcing in markets where job boards were not reaching qualified candidates.

Result

Interview-to-offer ratio came down from 5:1 to 3:1 for field and technical roles within 6 months. Fewer interviews, better candidates, faster fills.

Experian · 2021–2024
Global TA

Global Team Leadership: 200+ Annual Hires Across U.S. & International Markets

Context

Experian is a Fortune 500 data and financial services company with 23,000 employees across 32 countries, supporting clients in more than 100 countries. Led a team of 3 across GTM and Engineering hiring while personally closing complex technical roles across U.S. and international markets including Engineering Platform Directors, Cloud Infrastructure Managers, and senior GTM positions in a B2B SaaS environment.

SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
Employer Brand

Company's First Candidate Feedback Program and Employer Brand Strategy

Problem

SCS had no structured way to collect candidate feedback and no data to inform employer brand decisions. Glassdoor presence did not reflect the company's actual culture or growth trajectory. No visibility into where the candidate experience was breaking down.

Action

Launched the company's first candidate feedback program using SurveyMonkey, sent to candidates post-process regardless of outcome. Partnered with Marketing to analyze feedback, identify process bottlenecks, and translate findings into employer brand improvements including updated job descriptions, interview experience changes, and a proactive Glassdoor engagement strategy. Redesigned the career site covering mission, benefits language, and employer brand messaging.

Result

Measurable lift in Glassdoor rating year-over-year. First time leadership had structured data on candidate experience. Employer brand improvements informed by real applicant feedback rather than assumptions.
