📁 Case Studies

Four documented examples of real problems I identified and solved — with specific actions and measurable outcomes.

SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
ATS Migration: Greenhouse → iCIMS + Workday HRIS Integration
Systems
Problem
SCS Engineers had no real-time visibility into hiring pipeline health or retention data across 70+ offices and 44 states. Greenhouse was not integrated with their HRIS. Leaders were making workforce decisions without accurate data. Agency relationships had no accountability structure, costing $300K+ annually with no measurable ROI.
Action
Led the full transition from Greenhouse to iCIMS — configuration, data migration, and hiring manager training across all offices. Implemented Workday HRIS integration unifying hiring, onboarding, and retention data for the first time. Launched a revamped employee referral program and introduced SeekOut for passive sourcing to reduce agency dependency across 44 states.
Result
Agency spend reduced by $250K+. Leadership gained first-ever real-time pipeline and retention visibility. Internal sourcing through SeekOut and referrals became a meaningful channel across a national footprint.
SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
Intake Process Redesign: Interview-to-Offer Ratio from 5:1 to 3:1
Process
Problem
No structured intake process. Hiring managers submitted reqs without clear requirements, compensation alignment, or interview frameworks. Result: a 5:1 interview-to-offer ratio — five candidates going through the full process for every single hire. Expensive in time, hiring manager bandwidth, and candidate experience across a 70-office national firm.
Action
Redesigned the intake protocol end-to-end. Implemented mandatory kickoff calls before any req went live. Built standardized job brief templates capturing requirements, compensation range, non-negotiables, and interview format. Established 48-hour feedback SLAs with hiring managers. Introduced SeekOut for passive sourcing in markets where job boards weren't reaching qualified candidates.
Result
Interview-to-offer ratio came down from 5:1 to 3:1 for field and technical roles within 6 months. Fewer interviews, better candidates, faster fills.

Global TA

SCS Engineers · 2024–Present
Company's First Candidate Feedback Program — SurveyMonkey + Employer Brand
Employer Brand
Problem
SCS had no structured way to collect candidate feedback and no data to inform employer brand decisions. Glassdoor presence didn't reflect the company's actual culture or growth trajectory. No visibility into where the candidate experience was breaking down.
Action
Launched the company's first candidate feedback program using SurveyMonkey, sent to all candidates post-process regardless of outcome. Partnered with Marketing to analyze feedback, identify process bottlenecks, and translate findings into employer brand improvements — updated job descriptions, interview experience changes, and a proactive Glassdoor engagement strategy.
Result
Measurable increase in Glassdoor rating. First time leadership had structured data on candidate experience. Employer brand improvements informed by real applicant feedback rather than assumptions.
